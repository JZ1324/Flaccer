FAQ

Questions DJs actually ask.

Does Flaccer upload my music?

No. Analysis happens locally on your Mac.

Can it scan Rekordbox libraries?

Yes. Import Rekordbox XML and Flaccer resolves the referenced local audio files.

Does it support ALAC?

Yes. ALAC/M4A is treated like any other audio file: the verdict comes from spectral evidence.

Can I export results?

Yes. Export CSV with verdict, cutoff, confidence, sample rate, and file paths.
