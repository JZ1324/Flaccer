Built for large libraries

Drop once. Review what matters.

Flaccer queues your music first, keeps the first track focused, then analyzes files in the background so suspicious results are easy to filter, copy, reveal, tag, or export.

Trust surface

Spectrograms that explain the verdict.

Verdicts are based on high-frequency energy, cutoff behavior, upper-band emptiness, confidence, and sample-rate context. The graph is there so you can inspect the evidence instead of blindly trusting a label.
